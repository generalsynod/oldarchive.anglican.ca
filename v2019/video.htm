“Vision 2019 is an opportunity to say ‘here’s what I think our church needs to be about.’”

Dream baby dream

Footage from the Vision 2019 baby photo shoot, where several babies were photographed for a poster advertising Vision 2019, a church-wide exercise to discern, dream, and decide where God wants the Anglican Church of Canada to be by 2019. All Canadian Anglicans are invited to chime in on the two-part question, "Where is your church now and where do you want the Anglican Church of Canada to be by 2019?"

Join the conversation.

 

For more information or questions, email vision2019@national.anglican.ca
