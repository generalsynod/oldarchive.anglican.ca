ABC Publishing is part of the Communications and Information Resources Department, and describes itself as the voice of Canadian Anglicanism. They produce prayer books, hymnbooks, resources for parish programs, books for church leaders, as well as Path Books, which offer practical spirituality to enrich everyday living.
