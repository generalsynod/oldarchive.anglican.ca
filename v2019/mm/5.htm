The Five Marks of Mission:

5. To strive to safeguard the integrity of creation and sustain and renew the life of the earth

Click on the videos below to learn how this Mark of Mission informs the work of General Synod.


 Archdeacon John Robertson, national gift planning officer for the Department of Philanthropy, talks about how his work at General Synod connects with “safeguarding the integrity of creation.”
How can the work of a national office, located in downtown Toronto, “safeguard the integrity of creation and sustain and renew the life of the earth?” One way is through responsible lifestyle choices, and this video shows an example: the Primate, Archbishop Fred Hiltz, has committed to commute by Toronto transit.
