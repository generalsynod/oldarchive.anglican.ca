How can the work of a national office, located in downtown Toronto, “safeguard the integrity of creation and sustain and renew the life of the earth?” One way is through responsible lifestyle choices, and this video shows an example: the Primate, Archbishop Fred Hiltz, has committed to commute by Toronto transit.

On a larger scale, the Partners in Mission and Ecojustice committee of General Synod tackles larger systems that cause environmental destruction. They produce free resources like Just Living, which educate parishes on caring for creation. They also help coordinate the very popular, week-long Justice Camp. The 2007 camp was held in Victoria, B.C., and focused on environmental issues, including lands and forests, coastal waters, and transportation.
