PPT Slide

    “It is wrong – environmentally, economically, and morally – to engage in the large-scale trade of water. Water must never be regarded as a commodity for exchange in the international marketplace. An adequate supply of clean water for peoples’ daily living needs is a basic human right and is best protected by maintaining control of water in the public sector.”


    Maud Barlow, chair council of Canadians
