How to contact members of General Synod

      You may send telephone messages to the General Synod office staff at 519 884 7022; you can also reach synod delegates by e-mail at synod@national.anglican.ca. Messages will be given to delegates or posted on a General Synod notice board. They also have fax facilities: the fax number is 519 884 7021.

      Mail can be sent to members at the following address: Your name, General Synod of the Anglican Church, Conference Centre, Ron Eydt Village, University of Waterloo, Box 16610, Waterloo Ontario N2J 4C1

      In order to have greater input into the General Synod process there is an online discussion group regarding the material and subjects likely to come to General Synod. We invite you to participate in the discussion group via email.
