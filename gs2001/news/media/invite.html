To: All Media

From: Tony Whittingham / Communications Consultant

Re: Invitation to Attend 36th General Synod of Anglican Church

Date: 13 June 2001


The Primate and Officers of General Synod of the Anglican Church of Canada are pleased to invite Media participation in the forthcoming Anglican General Synod. You may do this either by joining us in person at Waterloo, Ontario (July 4 -11, 2001) - or, from the newsroom, through regular news updates on the General Synod website (www.anglican.ca/synod).

With this note are included three attachments to help inform you of the flow and potential newsworthiness of the proceedings of our triennial 8-day meeting, comprising nearly 500 church leaders and elected community delegates from across Canada:

  1. Media Advisory

  2. General Synod Daily Schedule

  3. Media Registration / Enquiry Form

Media representatives attending General Synod will have the use of dedicated facilities comprising computer hookups, work stations and interview areas. In addition, live television monitoring will be provided for all sessions from the plenary hall. Media representatives interested in covering General Synod are requested to complete the attached Media Registration Form (by "hard copy" fax or through the web). Upon receipt of registration, we will send copies to all accredited media of the "Convening Circular" (agenda and all supporting documents) as well as a Media Information Kit.

For specific enquiries, please contact us directly by telephone at 416-924-9199 (x324), by fax at 416-924-0211 or e-mail at awhittingham@national.anglican.ca.

For additional background on General Synod or any other aspect of the Anglican Church of Canada, please visit our website at www.anglican.ca.
