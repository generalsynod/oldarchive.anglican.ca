World Council of Churches
"Churches together for Peace and Justice in the Middle East”
Amman, Jordan, 17-21 June 2007

The Anglican Church of Canada, through its national Partners in Mission, EcoJustice and PWRDF programs, has a long history of commitment to partnership with churches and church councils in the Middle East towards peace with justice in the region. The Palestine Israel Ecumenical Forum (PIEF), to be launched in Amman, Jordan, June 17-21, 2007, initiates a process of renewed and broader ecumenical collaboration, commitment and action for peace with justice in Palestine and Israel.
