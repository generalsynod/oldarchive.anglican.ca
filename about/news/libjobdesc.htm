Librarian

Application is invited for the following appointment:

COLLEGE LIBRARIAN

A suitably qualified and experienced candidate is sought to lead and manage the overall development of the George Knight Library and Archives to meet the needs of the Pacific Theological College’s staff and on-campus and distance students in today’s challenging environment. 

The person appointed will be responsible for customer and technical services including the supervision and management of library staff. Duties include the development of the Library’s collection in various formats in consultation with the lecturers and faculty staff; the development of user education and information literacy programs; cataloguing and classification of library materials according to internationally accepted standards; the design and implementation of library policies, plans and procedures; the management of the Library’s budget; the maintenance of an Integrated Library System, Alice for Windows and the expansion of links with national, regional and international sources of information. The appointee will be responsible to the Principal and will have the assistance of the Library Advisory Committee.

Qualifications and Experience: Applicants must have a professional qualification in Library and Information Studies that is recognized by an accrediting body such as the Australian Library and Information Association (ALIA); a minimum of 3 years experience at the professional level in the following areas of library work: (i) library management in an academic or theological library (ii) application and maintenance of an integrated library system (iii) staff supervision, training and management; (iv) development of library policies (v) technical services and the application of international standards, namely AACR2, DDC and Sears List of Subject Headings (vi) familiarity with online databases, the Internet and Microsoft applications. The appointee should have leadership qualities and excellent interpersonal and communication skills and be prepared to work evening and weekend shifts.

Preference will be given to qualified applicants from the Pacific with a degree in theological studies or knowledge of French or a Pacific language and/or the ability to work in a multi-cultural environment. Applicants with experience in a public or school library will also be considered.


Salary, Terms and Conditions:

Salary: F$25,000.00 per annum.  The position is available from January 2008.  Applicants should include full curriculum vitae, endorsement letter by the Home Church or Mission Board, names and contact details of three referees, one of whom should be the current professional supervisor.

Applications should be addressed to Rev. Dr. Feleterika Nokise, Principal, Pacific Theological College, Private Mail Bag, Suva, Fiji. Applications may alternatively, or additionally, be submitted by fax (No. 679 3301728) or E-mail to principal@ptc.ac.fj from whom further details may be obtained.  Originals documents are required before an offer is made.

For information about PTC see website http://www.ptc.ac.fj
