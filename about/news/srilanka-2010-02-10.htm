Sri Lanka: Post Presidential Election Realities

Following recent Presidential elections in Sri Lanka, the following statement "Post Presidential Election Realities" was issued by Heads of Churches, including Bishops Duleep de Chickera and Kumara Illangesinghe, Church of Ceylon.

The (Anglican) Church of Ceylon, a long standing partner of the Anglican Church of Canada, is a leader among faith communities in Sri Lanka, seeking reconciliation and peace with justice in a country battered by many years of civil war. Though the end of this war in 2009 by military means brought a cessation of hostilities, much political work is now needed to ease peoples' suffering and bring about hope, especially among those who continue to be internally displaced in the north and east. Please pray for the church in Sri Lanka, and its leaders, for all communities of faith and those seeking the re-establishment of the rule of law, order and good government.
