Statement of Authorization and Commission for AICP to nominate a candidate for the position of the first National Indigenous Bishop

Sacred Circle 2005

We the Sacred Circle of 2005, meeting at Pinawa, Manitoba, having called on the Primate and the Anglican Church of Canada to provide a national aboriginal bishop within a year, authorize and commission, the Anglican Council of Indigenous Peoples (ACIP), to nominate to the Primate, a fit and qualified person to be appointed as the first National Indigenous Bishop.

Further we authorize and commission the Anglican Council of Indigenous Peoples to move the appropriate councils of Anglican Church of Canada

  1. to adopt a process for the election of subsequent National Indigenous Bishops by a Sacred Circle representative of ail Canadian Indigenous Anglicans, and
  1. to empower the National Indigenous Bishop with episcopal and pastoral responsibilities as well as full authority and jurisdiction for aboriginal communities across Canada.

August 12. 2005
