Anglican Indian and Eskimo Residential Schools

A

All Hallows School for Girls, Yale, BC, 1884-1917, [amalgamated with St.George’s Indian Residential School, Lytton, BC 1917,] Diocese of New Westminster, Diocese of Cariboo.

All Saints Indian Residential School, Lac La Ronge, SK, 1906-1947, [amalgamated 1948 with new Prince Albert Indian Residential School, a.k.a. All Saints School until 1953,] Diocese of Saskatchewan, Authorized Enrolment – 50 to 100.

All Saints Indian Residential School, Aklavik, NWT, 1936-1959, Diocese of Arctic, Authorized Enrolment – 100 to 150.

B

Bishop Horden Memorial School, Moose Factory, ON, [Moose Factory Indian Residential School, Moose Fort Indian Residential School, Horden Hall,] 1855-1969, Diocese of Moosonee, Authorized Enrolment – 30 to 250.

Bompas Hall Indian Residential School, Fort Simpson, NWT, 1960-1969 Diocese of Yukon, Authorized Enrolment – 50.

C

Chooutla Indian Residential School, Carcross, YT, [Carcross Indian Residential School,] 1903-1969, Diocese of Yukon, Authorized Enrolment – 40 to 135.

Coppermine Residential School, Coppermine, Nunavut, [Coppermine Tent Hostel,] 1955-1959, Diocese of the Arctic, Authorized Enrolment – 30 to 44.

E

Elkhorn Indian Residential School, Elkhorn, MB [Washakada Indian Residential School] 1888-1949, Diocese of Brandon, Authorized Enrolment – 70 to 130.

F

Fort McPherson Indian Residential School, Fort McPherson, NWT, [Fleming Hall,] 1951-1969, Diocese of the Arctic, Authorized Enrolment – 20 to 100.

G

Gordon’s Indian Residential School, Gordon’s Reserve, Punnichy, SK, 1888-1969, Diocese of Qu’Appelle, Authorized Enrolment – 15 to 165.

L

La Tuque Indian Residential School, La Tuque, QC, 1963-1969, Diocese of Quebec, Authorized Enrolment – 250.

M

Mackay Indian Residential School, The Pas, MB, 1915-1933, Diocese of Saskatchewan, Authorized Enrolment – 80.

Mackay Indian Residential School, Dauphin, MB, [Dauphin Mackay Indian Residential School, MB,] 1957-1969, Diocese of Brandon, Authorized Enrolment – 80 to 250.

Metlakatla Indian Residential School, Metlakatla, BC, ca.1870-ca.1878, Diocese of Caledonia.

Mohawk Institute, Brantford, ON, 1831-1969, Diocese of Huron, Authorized Enrolment – 15 to 185. *

O

Old Sun Indian Residential School, Blackfoot Reserve, Gleichen , AB, 1886-1969, Diocese of Calgary, Authorized Enrolment – 15 to 142.

P

Pelican Lake Indian Residential School, Sioux Lookout, ON, [Sioux Lookout Indian Residential School,] 1926-1969, Diocese of Keewatin, Authorized Enrolment – 135 to 160.

Prince Albert Indian Residential School, Prince Albert, SK, 1948-1969, [All Saints Indian Residential School, Lac La Ronge and St. Albans Indian Residential School, Prince Albert amalgamated to form Prince Albert Indian Residential School,] Diocese of Saskatchewan, Authorized Enrolment – 100 to 400.

R

Red River Settlement Boarding School, Fort Douglas (Winnipeg), MB, 1820–1833, Diocese of Rupert’s Land, Authorized Enrolment – 10.

S

Shingle Point Indian Residential School, Shingle Point, YT, 1929-1936, Diocese of Mackenzie River, Diocese of the Arctic, Authorized Enrolment – 25 to 40.

Shingwauk Indian Residential School, Sault Ste. Marie, ON, 1873-1969, Diocese of Algoma, Authorized Enrolment – 50 to 150.

St. Agnes Hostel, Whitehorse, YK, 1952-1966, Diocese of Yukon, Authorized Enrolment – 12 to 40.

St. Alban’s Indian Residential School, Prince Albert, SK, 1944-1951, [amalgamated 1951 with new Prince Albert Indian Residential School, Prince Albert, SK] Diocese of Saskatchewan, Authorized Enrolment – 100 to 200.

St. Andrew’s Indian Residential School, Whitefish Lake [Atikameg,] AB, 1903-1950, Diocese of Athabasca, Authorized Enrolment – 10 to 30.

St. Barnabas Indian Residential School, Onion Lake, SK, 1893-1943, (Onion Lake Indian Residential School,) [amalgamated with St. Alban’s Indian Residential School, Prince Albert, 1944,] Diocese of Saskatchewan, Authorized Enrolment – 60 to 90.

St. Barnabas Indian Residential School, Sarcee Reserve, AB, 1892-1922, Diocese of Calgary, Authorized Enrolment – 6 to 35.

St. Cyprian’s Indian Residential School, Brocket, AB [Peigan Indian Residential School,] 1890-1961, Diocese of Calgary, Authorized Enrolment – 12 to 60.

St. George’s Indian Residential School, Lytton, BC, [Lytton Indian Residential School,] 1902-1969, Diocese of Cariboo, Authorized Enrolment – 40 to 180. *

St. John’s Indian Residential School, Chapleau, ON [Chapleau Indian Residential School,] 1907-1948, Diocese of Moosonee, Authorized Enrolment – 18 to 100.

St. John’s Indian Residential School, Wabasca, AB [Wabasca Indian Residential School,] 1894-1966, Diocese of Athabasca, Authorized Enrolment – 15 to 60.

St. Michael’s Indian Residential School, Alert Bay, BC, [Alert Bay Indian Residential School,] 1882-1969, Diocese of British Columbia, Authorized Enrolment – 40 to 225.

St. Paul’s Indian Residential School, Blood Reserve,Cardston, AB [Blood Indian Residential School,] 1889-1969, Diocese of Calgary, Authorized Enrolment –25 to 200.

St. Paul’s Hostel, Dawson, YK, [St. Paul’s Hall] 1920-1952, Diocese of Yukon, Authorized Enrolment – 20 to 40.

St. Peter’s Indian Residential School, Hay River, NWT, 1895-1937, Diocese of the Arctic, Authorized Enrolment – 25 to 50.

St. Peter’s Indian Residential School, Lesser Slave Lake, AB, [Lesser Slave Lake Indian Residential School,] 1894-1932, Diocese of Athabasca, Authorized Enrolment – 20 to 35.

St. Philip’s Indian Residential School, Fort George, QC. [Fort George Indian Residential School,] 1933-1969, Diocese of Moosonee, Authorized Enrolment – 35 to 75.

Stringer Hall, Inuvik, NWT, 1959-1970, Diocese of Yukon, Authorized Enrolment – 250.

 

Note: * Residential Schools funded and administered by the New England Company.
