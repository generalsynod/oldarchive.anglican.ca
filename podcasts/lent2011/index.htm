Solemnity and Surprise

For Lent 2011, the Anglican Church of Canada offered a special series of podcasts: "A Journey from Lent to Easter—From Creation's Dust to Resurrection's Light," written and presented by Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert of the Sisters of Saint John the Divine (SSJD). The series includes nine podcasts, spanning from Ash Wednesday (March 9) through Lent to Easter Sunday (April 24).

Episodes

On Ash Wednesday, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, encourages listeners to consider that regardless of what they put off or take on during Lent, they are deeply loved by God. (Podcast 1 of 9)
Ash Wednesday: We are stardust

For the first week of Lent, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, examines Jesus's perseverance during his temptation in the desert. She challenges listeners to consider their own choices in the context of their baptismal promises. (Podcast 2 of 9)
Lent 1: Choices, choices

For the second week of Lent, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, shares stories of unconditional love and suggests ways to make space for love during the season of Lent. (Podcast 3 of 9)
Lent 2: Love is hard work

For the third week of Lent, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, explores the power of physical and spiritual thirst. (Podcast 4 of 9)
Lent 3: Living water

For the fourth week of Lent, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, wonders about our need for light and suggests ways that we can appreciate the gifts of light in our lives. (Podcast 5 of 9)
Lent 4: Longing for light

For the fifth week of Lent, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, unpacks stories of Ezekiel and Lazarus to share the revivifying power of God's spirit. (Podcast 6 of 9)
Lent 5: Breathing life into dry bones

On Palm Sunday, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, suggests ways that we pilgrims can find space for reflection during a busy liturgical season. (Podcast 7 of 9)
Palm Sunday: You move too fast

For Good Friday, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, shares a powerful poem about the nails that held Jesus to the cross. She also offers ways to ponder love on this sombre day. (Podcast 8 of 9)
Good Friday: God's meaning is love

In this final podcast, for Easter Sunday, Sister Elizabeth Ann Eckert, SSJD, describes the wondrous resurrection light that all Christians celebrate on this day. (Podcast 9 of 9)
Easter Sunday: Be in the light
