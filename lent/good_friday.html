A Prayer for Good Friday:
Almighty and everlasting God,
by whose Spirit the whole body of the Church is
    governed and sanctified:
Receive our supplications and prayers,
which we offer before thee for all estates of men in
    thy holy Church,
that every member of the same,
in his vocation and ministry,
may truly and godly serve thee;
through our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ.


—Book of Common Prayer, Anglican Church of Canada
