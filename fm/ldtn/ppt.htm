Powerpoint presentation on Letting Down the Nets to Diocean Finance Officers

Letting Down the Nets is a program and process about finding the resources and tools with which to do the work that God calls us to do together. Serving God's World, Stregthening the Church: A Framework for a common journey in Christ is a shared vision shaped by General Synod, 2004. It is a vision for growth in mission and ministry across Canada for the next six years. The challenge of making this vision and the Framework real lies in finding and coordinating the resources required for the new work along with our existing work. Letting Down the Nets describes four major initiatives along with a plan for funding each of them. The attached power point presentation provides a broader outline of what Letting Down the Nets is all about. It is being provided as a resource in response to requests that it be made available to present at various diocesan and parish events.
