In June 2004, General Synod approved the stewardship proposals contained in Letting Down the Nets. The Financial Development Sub-Committee of the Financial Management and Development Committee recognizes that based on conservative projections there is the potential for the church to increase its revenue by 10 to 15 per cent a year for several years through annual giving. Backed by the appropriate infrastructure and resources, the church could raise between $75 million and $100 million in the next 10 years through an intentional process of planned and major gifts.

Letting Down the Nets describes four major initiatives along with a plan for funding each of them. The sub-committee is now raising the money needed for the implementation of these four plans between 2005 and 2008.
