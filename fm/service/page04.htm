We propose a diocesan-based pilot initiative in stewardship education and financial development with the goal of increasing annual giving by eight to 10 per cent a year for several years. A key component of this initiative will be the promotion of proportional giving. Another will be creating an acceptance that stewardship education is a transformation process. A stewardship pilot project will be undertaken in at least four dioceses in the next three years. General Synod will assist by providing staff, resources and funding for diocesan training, guest speakers and workshop leaders.

Each pilot project will be for three years with the goal of becoming self-supporting. The cost for each pilot project will be $125,000 over the three-year period. In 2008, the process will begin again in four more dioceses. We estimate the total cost of this initiative over four years to be $665,000.
