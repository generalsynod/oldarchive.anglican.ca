The total cost of implementing Letting Down the Nets in the next four years is approximately $750,000 per year for a total estimated cost of $3 million. Donors for this initiative have already provided more than $150,000 and further donations are anticipated early in 2005.

Having embraced the vision for the church contained in the Framework it now falls on us to find the resources to make it all real. In the long term, we believe that the implementation of the Framework through the strategy described in this document will fulfil the document’s vision for growth in membership, faith and service in God’s world.
