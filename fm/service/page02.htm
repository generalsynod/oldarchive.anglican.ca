The Framework calls on the church to undertake several new initiatives by

  • Raising up a fresh and relevant vision and future for our church
  • Working together in a new spirit of partnership and shared resources
  • Developing diocesan-based stewardship and financial development pilot projects for raising new leaders and resources
  • Developing leaders
  • Ministering to youth and young adults
  • Enabling national and regional consultations on evangelism
  • Developing networks in parishes and dioceses.
