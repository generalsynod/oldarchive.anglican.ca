In the beginning it is about stewardship – about finding the resources and tools with which to do the work that God calls us to do together. Serving God’s World, Strengthening the Church: A Framework for a common journey in Christ is a shared vision shaped by General Synod. It is a vision for growth in mission and ministry across Canada for the next six years, a vision for service in God’s world. The challenge of making this vision and the Framework real lies in finding and coordinating the resources required for the new work along with our existing work.

We envision a church that is growing in membership, faith and service in God’s world – a church that reaches out to serve the world God loves by:

  • Inviting people into a relationship with Jesus Christ
  • Encouraging spiritual growth and development in all its members
  • Welcoming and supporting children and young people in their journey of faith
  • Nurturing and strengthening local Christian communities in their worship, learning and mission
