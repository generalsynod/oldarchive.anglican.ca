Observers information and registration and at General Synod

We appreciate your interest in The Anglican Church of Canada and hope that the following will be helpful and make your time with us more enjoyable.

For those who are not attending the Synod will be broadcast on our website www.anglican.ca

Observer registration for General Synod will be at 9:00 a.m. 2:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. each day in the Lobby of the Marlborough Hotel, 331 Smith Street, Winnipeg, Manitoba.

Exceptions to the above are:

Tuesday, June 19 Opening Service  7:30 p.m.
Cathedral of St. John, 135 Anderson Ave.
No registration required.  Seating limited.
View at www.anglican.ca
Thursday, June 21 Winnipeg Convention Centre
Synod will meet with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in Canada
The session may be viewed at www.anglican.ca but is not open to observers.
Friday, June 22 Primatial Election 
Registration begins at 8:30 a.m. at Holy Trinity Church, 256 Smith Street
View at www.anglican.ca
Sunday, June 24 Dinner at the Radisson Hotel but not open to observers.
Monday, June 25 Closing Service and Installation of New Primate
All are welcome, no registration required.
View at www.anglican.ca

Seating on the floor of Synod is limited to 30 persons and will be on a first come first seated basis. A room has been designated on the 9th Floor of the Hotel for observers. The proceedings of Synod will be shown on two large screens located in the room.

We encourage you to visit our displays at four locations throughout the hotel: Lobby; Oxford Hall (Mezzanine); Skyview Foyer and Marlborough Room.

If you wish to purchase a meal while at Synod, Joanna’s Café in the lobby is open and a list of nearby restaurants is available from the Information Desk in the Lobby.

Should you need assistance while at Synod, staff and volunteers (wearing “ASK ME” buttons) will be available to answer your questions.
