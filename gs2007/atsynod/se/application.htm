How to submit your work

To submit a .jpeg photograph of your work, please send a CD containing the high resolution image to:

Anglican Video
80 Hayden St.,
Toronto, ON
M4Y 2J6

The deadline for submissions is March 1st, 2007. We will not be able to return your CD to you. The CD must be accompanied by an application form and a signed authorization with your permission to show your work during General Synod and to publish the photograph in an accompanying booklet, available at General Synod. You will be notified by e-mail or letter about whether or not your piece is being shown during General Synod.

All art submissions will remain the property of the submitting artists.

  • Download the application (PDF format)

Accompanying Booklet

Sacred Expressions will be producing a small booklet featuring the photographs of the selected pieces of art, along with the name of the artist.

Each artist has the option to include his or her contact information. This option may be a desirable one for artists who wish to sell the selected piece or other pieces. If you wish to include your contact information, please indicate this on the application form.

The booklet will be available during General Synod and for at least one year after General Synod through Anglican Video.
