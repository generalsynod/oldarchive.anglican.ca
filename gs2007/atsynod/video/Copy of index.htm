General Synod 2007 live coverage

Through the auspices of Anglican Video, the full proceedings of General Synod 2007 will be web cast through this site. The web cast will include all sessions in the plenary hall, as well as the opening service and election of a new primate.

Daily summary programs produced by Anglican Video will be available for viewing throughout the day. Highlights will be archived daily.

Whenever a session of General Synod is held, we will be web casting. The General Synod agenda and the Daily Reports lists the times (Central Daylight Time) that General Synod is in session, and gives a guide to the topics being discussed.

Technical issues with the webcast

Due to a hardware malfunction that occurred as the opening service was beginning, the webcast of the service was not of the quality in video or audio planned. We believe the problem has been identified, and staff will be working over the next day to fix this problem. Thank you for your patience in this matter.

 

 Video Status:

Videos are streamed through an Adobe Flash player. If you are having problems, please visit our help page.
