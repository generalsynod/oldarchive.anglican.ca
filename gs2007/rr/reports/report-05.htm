To the Members of the General Synod 

I have the honour to report that the following consecrations and installations were recorded during the period from March 1, 2004 to February 28, 2007:

Consecrations

March 25, 2004 Susan Elizabeth Moxley as a Suffragan Bishop in the Diocese of Nova Scotia and Prince Edward Island
May 16, 2004 Gordon Stanley Light as a Bishop Suffragan to the Metropolitan of the Ecclesiastical Province of British Columbia and Yukon with responsibility for the Anglican Parishes of the Central Interior
September 21, 2004 Cyrus Clement James Pitman as Bishop Coadjutor in the Diocese of Eastern Newfoundland and Labrador
October 14, 2004 Barry Bryan Clarke as the 11th Bishop of the Diocese of Montreal
February 5, 2005 Philip Poole as a Suffragan Bishop in the Diocese of Toronto
September 17, 2005 John Elswood Privett as the 9th Bishop of the Diocese of Kootenay
November 30, 2005 David Torraville as the 4th Bishop of the Diocese of Central Newfoundland
March 25, 2006 Patrick Yu as a Suffragan Bishop in the Diocese of Toronto
May 23, 2006 Gregory Kerr-Wilson as the 11th Bishop of the Diocese of Qu’Appelle
September 29, 2006 Derek Balfour Erskine Hoskin as the 9th Bishop of the Diocese of Calgary

Installations

June 4, 2004 Andrew Sandford Hutchison as the 12th Primate of the Anglican Church of Canada
September 12, 2004 Colin Robert Johnson as the 11th Bishop of the Diocese of Toronto
September 26, 2004 Bruce Stavert as the 21st Metropolitan of the Ecclesiastical Province of Canada
November 9, 2004 Caleb Lawrence as the 17th Metropolitan of the Ecclesiastical Province of Ontario
December 5, 2004 Cyrus Clement James Pitman as the 4th Bishop of the Diocese of Eastern Newfoundland and Labrador
February 13, 2005 Peter R. Coffin as the 9th Bishop Ordinary to the Canadian Forces
November 5, 2005 Terrence Owen Buckle as the 10th Metropolitan of the Ecclesiastical Province of British Columbia and Yukon

March 1, 2007
Ronald C. Stevenson, Chancellor
