Love Life: A Lenten video series

  • Love Life: Living the Gospel of Love – Yes

Receive video devotionals straight to your inbox, beginning Ash Wednesday, March 5.

Produced by the brothers of the Society of St. John the Evangelist (SSJE) for the Anglican Church of Canada, this series will be your daily companion through the Lenten season.

The Gospel of John is the guiding document for communal life among the SSJE brothers. Follow along as members of their community share their enthusiasm for and insight into Jesus’ message of Life—a message that can “unlock our hearts and transform our lives.”

I came that they may have life, and have it abundantly. – John 10:10, NRSV

Subscribe online now.

For ideas on how to promote and use these videos in your parish, visit the Love Life: Living the Gospel of Love resources page. Companion resources include weekly compilation videos, bulletin inserts, and a workbook suited for personal or group devotional use.
