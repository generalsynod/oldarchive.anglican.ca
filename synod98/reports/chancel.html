#026

REPORT OF THE CHANCELLOR

 

To Members of the General Synod:

 

I wish to report the following consecrations and installations since June 1995:

 

Consecrations

 

June 2, 1996 Paul Idlout as Suffragan Bishop of The Arctic

Sept. 21, 1997 Ralph Spence as the Co-adjutor Bishop of Niagara

Sept. 22, 1997 Ann Tottenham as the Suffragan Bishop of Toronto

Sept. 29, 1997 Leonard Whitten as the Co-adjutor Bishop of Western Newfoundland

Jan. 24, 1998 Duncan Wallace as the 10th Bishop of Qu’Appelle

 

Installations

 

Sept. 17, 1995 Terrence Buckle as the 10th Bishop of the Yukon

Dec. 8, 1996 Gordon Beardy as the 8th Bishop of Keewatin

May 31, 1997 Victoria Matthews as 9th Bishop of the Diocese of Edmonton

Nov. 2, 1997 Leonard Whitten as the 3rd Bishop of Western Newfoundland

Nov. 30, 1997 Andrew Hutchison as Bishop Ordinary to the Canadian Forces

January 1, 1998 Russell Hatton as Assistant Bishop of the Diocese of Montreal

Jan. 6, 1998 Arthur Peters as 19th Metropolitan of Canada

Jan. 11, 1998 Ralph Spence as the 11th Bishop of Niagara

 

Note: William Hockin will be consecrated Co-adjutor Bishop of Fredericton on June 24, 1998.
