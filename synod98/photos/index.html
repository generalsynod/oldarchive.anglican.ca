Day 1: May 21 1998


Still setting up the floor of Plenary at 9am, May 21.  The tables at the back of the room on the left will be the podium where the Primate will preside over the session.



TV producer Lisa Barry (in jeans) and General Synod Coordinator Jamie Tomlinson (back facing camera) consult in front of TV production unit.  Photo taken 9:30am May 21.



At the back of the Plenary floor are displays from church committees and related groups.   They are spending the day setting up their displays.


Television production crew setting up studio lights on the floor in preparation for the dry run later tonight.


One of the shuttle van arriving at the Registration centre with members coming from the airport.  Several shuttle vans are in operation throughout the Synod.
