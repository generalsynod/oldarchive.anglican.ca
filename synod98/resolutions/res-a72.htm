Resolution Number: A-72

Subject: Amendment to Canon XI - Anglican Book Centre

Moved By: (Name) Hon. Ron Stevenson from the Diocese of Fredericton

Seconded By: (Name) Archdeacon Jim Boyles, General Secretary

Note: The mover and the seconder must be members of the General Synod and be present in the House when the resolution is before the synod for debate.

 

BE IT RESOLVED:

That Canon XI be amended

i) by deleting sections 1, 2 and 4

ii) by deleting the words "and in consultation with the Advisory Board" in section 3

iii) in section 5, add the words "of Information Resources" after the word "Director"

iv) renumber the sections accordingly.

 

 

 

 

EXPLANATORY NOTE/BACKGROUND INFORMATION:

If the change in the structure of the Information Resources Committee (Resolution A-25) is approved, this resolution will be presented to General Synod to bring the Canon in line with the change in the Constitution. It will eliminate the Anglican Book Centre Advisory Board and transfer its functions to the Information Resources Committee.
