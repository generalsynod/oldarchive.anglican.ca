Diocese of Toronto

The following action from the Executive, at a meeting held January 10, 1998, is reported:

7. The New Hymn Book

That a "words only" edition of the new hymn book be produced as soon as possible after the release date of the "music" edition.

Rationale

To allow for easy use by both seniors and young people in parishes where weight is an issue. It would also allow for larger words to be printed for those who have vision difficulties.
