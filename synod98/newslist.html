Synod98 Direct
General Synod Newswire
and nightly Perspectives interactive newsletter

To help you stay up to date on happenings at General Synod, Synod98 Direct will send you occasional news releases on developments as they occur.  As well, each night, you will receive Perspectives, a short interactive newsletter
that captures the colour, not just the news, of Synod.

It is not a substitute for this web site.  You are encouraged to bookmark this site, as more frequent updates will happen there, plus live photos, audio, and TV updates.  You'll also find features and interviews on the web site, and a complete list of all reports and resolutions (and their updated status on the floor of Plenary).

Synod98 Direct is "read-only" meaning only Synod98 newsroom staff can use the list to broadcast information.  If you would like to contact the staff, email owner-acc-synodnews@anglican.ca

Synod98 Direct (this mailing list) and Synod98 Live! (the web site) is written and coordinated onsite by Tod Maffin under the direction of Sam Carriere and support from Lorie Chortyk and Joan Shanks.

Here's how to join:
Send an email to majordomo@ns1.anglican.ca and put the words subscribe acc-synodnews in the body of your message.   As easy as that!  You'll get a confirmation email back in seconds.

(If you have any problems, just send an email to owner-acc-synodnews@anglican.ca)
