Jaime Cardinal Ortega of Cuba with Archbishop Andrew Hutchison.

 

Archbishop Andrew Hutchison and Alexandra Bugailiskis, Canadian Ambassador to Cuba.

Archbishop Andrew Hutchison meets with Anglican students at an ecumenical seminary in Matanza, Cuba.
