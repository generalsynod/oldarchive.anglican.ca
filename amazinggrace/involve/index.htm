Start planning your Amazing Grace event today. All you need are a singer (or 100!), and if you have one, a recording device to capture the moment. (Wondering how to get a video? Click here.)

Here are some questions to get the creative juices flowing:

  • What musical resources do we have in our parish (vocal, instrumental, rhythmic)?

  • Is there an interesting location inside or outside our church where we could film this?

  • How can we draw on skills from people of different ages?

  • Is there a unique cultural angle we could take on the hymn? (What about “Amazing Grace” in Cantonese? Or sung to a reggae beat?)
