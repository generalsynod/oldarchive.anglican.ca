Amazing Grace Facebook Group

If you're a member of Facebook, the social networking website, you can sign up for the "Amazing Grace Hymn Sing" event. Just search "Amazing Grace Hymn Sing" on Facebook and you will find the event listed within the Council of the North group. Once you're there, you can post messages on the public message board and connect withother singers across Canada.
