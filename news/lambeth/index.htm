Lambeth 2008

Lambeth Conference Prayer
Pour down upon us, O God, the gifts of your Holy Spirit, that those who prepare for the Lambeth Conference may be filled with wisdom and understanding. May they know at work within them that creative energy and vision which belong to our humanity, made in your image and redeemed by your love, though Jesus Christ our Lord. Amen

The Lambeth Conference is the once-a-decade meeting of all bishops in the worldwide Anglican Communion for study, prayer, and fellowship. This year’s conference theme is “equipping bishops for mission” and it will run from July 16 to Aug. 3 in Canterbury, England.

This website is a hub for news and commentary on the Lambeth Conference, with a particular Canadian perspective.
